All-in-One Super App Plan

“One App. Every Service. Every City.”
by Arsalan Shaikh

🚫 The Problem

Users need multiple apps for food, salon, groceries, repairs, and more. This creates confusion, storage issues, and time loss. No app offers everything in one place.

✅ The Solution

Build a single app that offers 30+ home services like food delivery, salon at home, electrician, security, plumber, grocery delivery, etc. One super app to solve all daily needs.

📊 Market Opportunity

  • 80 Cr+ Internet users in India
  • 25 Cr+ households
  • Tier 2 & Tier 3 cities need this the most
  • No direct competitor offering all-in-one model

💡 Features

  • Customer app: Book, pay, track services
  • Vendor dashboard: Accept orders, earn
  • Admin panel: Manage cities, users, payments

💰 Business Model

  • 10–20% commission per service
  • Delivery fee (₹10–₹40)
  • Vendor subscriptions (Prime, Boosted)
  • In-app advertising for local businesses

🆚 Competitive Advantage

Swiggy = Food only
Urban Company = Salon + plumber only
Zepto = Grocery only
Our App = ALL services in ONE

🚀 Launch Plan

  • Start in Sangamner + Akole (pilot)
  • Use WhatsApp, posters, social media to onboard vendors
  • Referral rewards for users
  • Scale to 10 cities in 6 months

🧠 Tech Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript
  • Backend: Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Hosting)
  • Payments: Razorpay
  • PWA App (convert website into mobile app)

🎯 Vision & Goal

Make India's small cities smarter with digital home services. Create jobs, build local trust, and reduce user stress. Goal: ₹1000 Cr company in 5 years, all self-funded.

📈 Timeline to Billionaire

  • 1st year: MVP, 2 cities, ₹10L–₹1Cr
  • 2–3 years: Expand to 50 cities, ₹10–50 Cr
  • 4–6 years: PAN India launch, ₹500–1000 Cr valuation

🧾 Budget

  • Domain: ₹500/year
  • Firebase: Free tier
  • Hosting: Free
  • Marketing: Posters, social, ₹2000/month
  • Total monthly cost ≈ ₹3000 max
